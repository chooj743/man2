Fungus Series • 2008 – 2016

Studio Moniker is an interactive design studio currently run by Luna Maurer and Roel Wouters, often uses Conditional Design practices commercially. In one of the projects, Fungus Series, it requires the participants to connect their given stickers onto another by its ends. This activity encourages participants to think creatively while involving them in the making process as co-authors.

Due to its conditions, it is inevitable that participants are required to reflect and respond to existing stickers that have already been placed before them. They are to consider their actions, to copy or not, to react in contrast, or perhaps take part in a pattern (Armstrong and Stojmirovic 136). Therefore, it is nearly impossible for participants to achieve arbitrary outcomes as it is required of them to relate to others.

Knots workshop • 2009 – 2013

Back in 2009, this workshop was first formulated by the Conditional Design team with a current total of three iterations. The latest version of Knots can be found in the Conditional Design Workbook, published in 2013. The conditions of the following workshops are adapted from ‘Workshop VIII, Knots,’ in Conditional Design Workbook, 2013, conducted by Jodi Choo along with respective participants.
